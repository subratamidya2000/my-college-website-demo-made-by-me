about us

JIS College of Engineering is an engineering college located in West Bengal, India. The college was established in 2000.The Institution is declared Autonomous by the University Grants Commission in 2011. It is affiliated to West Bengal University of Technology. it is situated in 2000. it is the 1st college of jis group.
